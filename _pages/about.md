Hi! I am a first-year PhD student at the [Department of Management Science and Engineering](https://msande.stanford.edu/), Stanford University. 

Prior to Stanford, I obtained my MMath in Mathematics and Statistics (integrated Bachelor's + Master's) from [Lincoln College](https://lincoln.ox.ac.uk/), University of Oxford. I am very grateful to have worked with [Prof. Seth Flaxman](https://sethrf.com/).

I am broadly interested in applications of optimal transport methods, the theory of diffusion models, stochastic control, and continuous time reinforcement learning methods.
